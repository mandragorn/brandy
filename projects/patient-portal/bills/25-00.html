    Request New Appointments

    Appointment requests through Patient Connect are quick and easy. Just choose the desired service, fill out a short pre-scheduling form, and choose from a calendar of available appointment times - all without picking up the phone. The hospital then confirms your appointment by email.

    Register for Appointments

    Patient Connect lets you register in advance for your appointments, saving time and repeat work. The registration wizard guides you through all the steps that need to be completed prior to each appointment, saving your data as you progress. After completing all the steps, you receive a bar code for fast and easy check-in at one of the on-site kiosks.
